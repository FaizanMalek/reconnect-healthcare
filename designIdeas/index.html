Our Philosophy

Health that is collaborative, whole, and deeply personal.

ReConnect Health offers a wide range of services including acupuncture, pelvic floor physiotherapy, orthopaedic physiotherapy, breast health physiotherapy, psychology, massage therapy, and private gynecological services.

We also provide engaging workshops, therapeutic group exercise, a caring community, and more — because we believe healing happens together.
